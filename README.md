*Sales Performance Dashboard & Business Insights*
Project Overview:

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover revenue trends, profitable product categories, and regional performance. 
The objective is to provide business insights that support data-driven decision-making and improve sales strategy.

Dataset-
The dataset used in this project contains retail order records including sales, profit, category, region, and customer segments.

Project Objectives:

-Analyze overall sales and profit performance
-Identify top-performing regions and product categories
-Evaluate monthly sales trends
-Discover high-profit and low-profit product segments
-Generate actionable business insights

*Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)*

Total Sales: ₹2.29M
Total Profit: ₹286K
Profit Margin: 12.5%
Total Orders: 9,994
Average Order Value: ₹229

*Analysis & Insights*

-West region generates the highest revenue.
-Technology category delivers the strongest profit margins.
-Sales peak during November and December due to holiday demand.
-Some furniture products show high sales but low profitability.
-Corporate customers contribute significantly to revenue.

*Tools & Skills Used*

-Data Cleaning & Preparation
-Data Analysis
-KPI Reporting
-Business Insight Generation
-Excel / Power BI (for dashboard & visualization)

This analysis helps businesses:

-Monitor sales performance
-Improve inventory planning
-Focus on high-margin products
-Optimize regional sales strategy
